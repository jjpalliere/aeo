01 / What

Brand visibility in AI search

AEO audits how three major AI assistants respond to queries relevant to your brand. It simulates real user questions across awareness, consideration, and decision stages, then extracts every brand mention, ranking position, and citation URL from the responses.

The output is a structured dataset: who gets mentioned, how often, in what position, and with what sources. No guesswork, no opinions — just the raw signal of how AI sees your competitive landscape.

02 / Why

AI is the new search surface

When someone asks an AI for a product recommendation, the response is the entire result page. There are no ten blue links — there is one synthesized answer. Brands that appear capture intent. Brands that don't, disappear.

AEO makes this visible: it quantifies where your brand stands relative to competitors in AI-generated responses, tracks how that position shifts across funnel stages and buyer personas, and identifies the content gaps that determine who gets cited.

03 / How

Audit workflow

Step 01
Add brand
Provide your URL. The system crawls your site, identifies your brand, and maps your competitive landscape automatically.
Step 02
Generate prompts
AEO produces ~60 search queries distributed across funnel stages and buyer personas. Review and approve before running.
Step 03
Run audit
Each prompt is sent to Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini. Responses are parsed for brand mentions, rankings, and citation URLs.
Step 04
Analyze results
Dashboard surfaces rankings, citation sources, competitor overlap, and funnel breakdowns. A chat assistant lets you query the data directly.
